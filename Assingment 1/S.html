Nike Air Force 1

This is a classic colorway that has been around for years. Recently it has made a comeback as it has exploded in popularity among students. I personally like this shoe because of its simplicity, wide range of colorways, and ability to go with any outfit.

Nike Dunk Panda

This is another colorway that has become very popular recently its a simple black on white shoe. Its a simple yet district colorway that is everywhere around campus. While this shoe isn't quite as popular as the Air Force 1's it takes a close second. I like this shoe because of the versatility when it comes to styling and its colorway.

Jordan 1 Fragment x Travis Scott

This is a recently released shoe that has been very difficult to get with resellers selling them for $2000+. However if you were lucky when these came out you could get them for $200. This Jordan 1 has the signature Travis Scott backwards swoosh and a unique colorway. I like this shoe because of its unique look and also because its a high top.

Jordan 4 Off-White Sail

This is another exclusive shoe that is extremely difficult to get in 2023 as it was last released in 2020 and has gone up in price to over $2000+. A unique thing about this shoe is that it was only made in womens sizing and it was in collaborating with Off-White. I like this shoe for its design and exclusivity.

Jordan 1 Chicago

This is another Jordan 1 but this time featuring the Chicago colorway. This is one of the oldest Jordan 1 colorways and has the most history. This shoe has been re-released several times with minor changes and continue to be one of the most influential colorways. I like this shoe because of its history and look.

Timberland 6-Inch Premium Boots Black

This is definitely an outlier compared to the other shoes here. However this is the shoe to wear when the wear conditions aren't good and you need something warm yet stylish. This is a boot that has a lot of reputation in pop culture and are often refereed to as Timbs. I like this shoe because its comfortable once broken in and its the ideal winter footwear.
